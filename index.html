Rana Soliman is a passionate multidisciplinary Toronto based designer in her fourth-year of the Bachelor of Design (Honours) program at York University and Sheridan College. She uses design as a catalyst for listening, problem solving, and connecting the world around her.

Rana's site is currently in the works, so why not have a look at her Behance in the interim, connect on LinkedIn, shoot an Email, or chat on Twitter. You can also visit Rana during her design grad show, The Next.
